Gangl, Design Case Study

in 2017, 90% of students reported that they were stressed and overworked during their post-secondary education. the Gangl App, is a platform designed and prototyped to combat stress caused by workload in undergraduate students.

The app was designed and prototyped using marvel design studio. the project followed the iterative design process to the book. it begain with the definition of a problem statement, identificaiton of key stakeholders and users and intensive reseach. A QFD and various other design tools were used to quantify specific aspects of the design. finally the project was iterativly designed from low fidelity, through high fidelity prototypes. It was presented as a final high fidelity prototype at the end of the term. Interested in learning more? View the full report.
